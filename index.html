Summon Griffin the Great

Griffon the Great is the king of No Man's Land in Stick War: Legacy. Later, he could be bought by the Player at the Shop as a spell to serve their purposes in battle. His size and ability in combat is comparable to the Final Boss, but can only be purchased for 1,000 gems. This alone relegates Griffon to the most important of battles, such as The Last Stand. Even if they are gigantic and powerful, in the hands of an inexperienced user, this beast has his limits.

Appeareance


During the early stages of the game, griffon looked exactly like the Boss giant of Stick War 1. The current Griffon the Great is similar to its old giant counterpart except it wields a magmatic club, wears a mask and has bigger proportions.

Abilities


Earthquake

The sole ability of Griffon: he is capable of stomping his feet, causing a gorge that stuns and damages a unit in a straight line, This ability is shared with the Boss Giant and a giant wearing a skin. Unlike other giants wearing a skin,Griffon can cause an earthquake on his own.
